What it means:

From IT World CyberSecurity is a term broadly applied to prevention of and preparedness for attacks on networks and computers, including data breaches, hacking attacks, phishing and social engineering, as well as threats to critical infrastructure

From The Department of Homeland Security Cyberspace and its underlying infrastructure are vulnerable to a wide range of risk stemming from both physical and cyber threats and hazards. Sophisticated cyber actors and nation-states exploit vulnerabilities to steal information and money and are developing capabilities to disrupt, destroy, or threaten the delivery of essential service"
